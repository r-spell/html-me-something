Adventures in Stenciling

I have been stenciling since I was in high school (so about 15 years). Most of that time I was doing stencils, by hand, with contact paper and x-acto knives. My earliest works were generally just black and white and were based on hand-drawn images or images altered with GIMP. Over the years I have done more with multicolor prints with stencils. I use GIMP and Inkscape to help me design stencils which make up different color layers to create a multicolor print. In the past year, I have also started using a laser cutter to make stencils. I belong to Arch Reactor, a community space for makers, which has a laser cutter members can use.
